TRM / AIG data analysis

The dataset consists of 25,811 number of cases over the span of 10 years, from Oct. 2011 to Nov. 2021.

Case Duration

Most commonly, cases last between 2 to 8 months. 72% of cases are closed within a year. 91% of cases are closed within 2 years.

Average Cost By Case, By Month

When comparing the "Total Claims Paid Amount With Recovery" by the duration of the case, the cost increases linearly with months. But when cases last for more than 2 years, the cost varies a lot without a clear trend.
